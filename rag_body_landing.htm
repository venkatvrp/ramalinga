About our Home

Ramalinga Adigalar Gurukulam chartered trust Registerd by Director of Social Welfare under JJ Act 2000 as amended in 2000(SI No.591/DSW/2011) is a social service organization started for disadvantaged, differently abled children and elderly people.

At present, we have 25 children and 10 elders at our home in the age group of 10 and 65 years. Mr.H.Ganapathy Pandian is founder of the organization. All the organizational activities are executed through the contributions from volunteers and donors. We have 10 staffs to take care of the Gurukulams daily needs.
